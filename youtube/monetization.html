YouTube Monetization Requirements

To reach monetization,there are a few requirements that need to be met on youtube. The following are the requirements for the different video formats done on youtube.These requirements include the following;

  • Long-form Videos: 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours
  • Shorts: 1,000 subscribers and 3 million views in the last 3 months
